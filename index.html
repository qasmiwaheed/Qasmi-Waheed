Maulana Waheed Qasmi

Professional Calligrapher at Abdullah bin Zaid Islamic Cultural Center

A distinguished Maulana and graduate of Darul Uloom Deoband, India, Waheed Qasmi brings a unique blend of spiritual scholarship and artistic mastery to the world of Arabic script. With over 15 years of professional experience, he currently serves as a Professional Calligrapher (Khattat) and Tutor in Doha, Qatar, dedicating his craft to the preservation and teaching of classical Islamic arts.

Calligraphy Education

Learn the sacred art of the pen under the guidance of a professional Tutor from the Abdullah bin Zaid Islamic Cultural Center. Courses are designed for all skill levels.

  • Basic Level: Understanding the anatomy of letters and the 'Nuqta' system.
  • Intermediate Level: Mastering complex connections and flow.
  • Advanced Level: Creating professional-grade compositions and artistic layouts.
