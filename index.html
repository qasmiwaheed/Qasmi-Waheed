The Art of the Sacred Pen

A graduate of the prestigious Darul Uloom Deoband, Maulana Waheed Qasmi beautifully bridges traditional Islamic scholarship with the aesthetic visual elegance of Arabic Calligraphy.

With over 15 years of absolute creative dedication, his compositions transform classic parchment into windows of deep spiritual reflection.

Classical Training

Preserving traditional frameworks

1. The Foundation

Mastering the anatomy of the hand-carved organic bamboo 'Qalam', learning paper preparation, and managing traditional ink consistency.

2. The Discipline

Immersing into the absolute precision of the structural Nuqta (dot) measurement framework system across Thuluth, Naskh, and Diwani styles.

3. Master Compositions

Progressing into fluid complex geometric structural configurations, large scale wall layouts, and complex architectural integrations.
